Product Requirements (MVP)

Goal
- Build a Nigeria-focused dating site with religion and ethnicity preferences.

Core Sections
- Profile
- People
- Liked You
- Chats

Account
- Sign up with first name, last name, phone, email, and profile photo.
- Sign in with email + password.

Profile Details
- Photos and videos
- Bio
- About you: work, education, gender, location, hometown, ethnicity, height,
  exercise, drink, smoking, looking for, family plans, have kids, star sign,
  politics, religion, pronouns, languages, connected accounts (music),
  my causes and communities, qualities I value

Discovery
- Filters by religion and ethnicity.
- Support all states in Nigeria.

Opening Moves
- Suggestions for questions to start chats.
